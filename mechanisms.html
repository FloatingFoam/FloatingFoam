I will explain 3 main categories of blasters here: Spring, Flywheel and AEG. Below are examples. When you finish reading, you should be able to tell apart the three.

There are 2 main types of nerf blasters: the ones powered by flywheels and the ones that are powered by springs. There are also special nerf blasters that aren't very common called AEG, which is a combination of motor and spring.
